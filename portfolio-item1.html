PredictingCredit Card Default

An ML approach to credit risk

According to Federal Reserve Economic Data, credit card delinquency rates have been increasing since 2016.

When a customer defaults on a credit card, or any loan, the bank eats the loss. Efficiently predicting who is likely to default can be an tool for credit risk management.

I use several tools, some of which are Python, sk-learn and matplotlib. Click "Go to Article" for details.
